Pills

Pills are themed, easier ways of getting certain potion effects. However, you can only eat them while you are hungry. There are currently 3 types of pills, listed bellow.

Health Pill

Refills Health Fully And Gives Regeneration

Yeet Pill

Throws You High Into The Sky

Mobility Pill

Gives Jump Boost And Speed For Quick Mobility
