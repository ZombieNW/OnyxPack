Able Sisters Disc

Able Sisters is one of the three Music Discs added to Onyxpack.

Credit

This song was originally composed by Nintendo for Animal Crossing: Wild World. The Onyxpack version is a cover of this song sequenced by David Josu and mastered by ZombieNW. If any of these people/groups would like the song to be removed from Onyxpack, that action will be performed immediately.

Aquiring

You can find Able Sisters discs as you would most other discs, getting a skeleton to kill a creeper. A music disc farming tutorial is here. A simple diagram bellow also shows the basic process.
